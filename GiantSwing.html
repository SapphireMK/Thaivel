The Giant Swing(Sao Ching cha)

  • The giant swing serve as a vivid symbol of the center of the old town in Bangkok.

  • Also it was used to celebrate southern Brahmin's swinging ceremony to Siva

  • This involved teams of men who would ride the swing on arcs as high as 25 meters above the ground and use their teeth to grab a bag of silver coins that hung in the air. However it was discontinued due to safety measures.
