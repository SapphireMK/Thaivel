Wat Pho

  • Wat Pho is known as Memory of the world by UNESCO as it was the first public university in Thailand. It was built during the reign of King Rama III. the The complex comprises of 4 chapels which have 394 gilded Buddha images. It is a national headquarters for teaching and preserving of traditional Thai medicine :Thai massage. The surrounding of the temple is also surrounded by massages school.
